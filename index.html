Know Your Rights

What is Workplace Harassment?

Unwelcome behavior including verbal, physical, or psychological misconduct affecting dignity and safety at work.

POSH Act Overview

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment Act ensures safe workplace environments and legal protection for all employees.
